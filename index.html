Know Kebele 01 - Official & Community Info

Geography: Kebele 01 is situated in the eastern part of Dodola Woreda, characterized by rolling hills and fertile valleys. It serves as a gateway to the Bale Mountains National Park.

History: Established in the early 1980s, Kebele 01 has a rich history tied to agricultural development in the region.

Infrastructure:

  • Electricity: Available to 70% of households.
  • Water: Access to communal boreholes and a new piped water project.
  • Internet: Limited 3G coverage, primarily via Ethio Telecom.
  • Healthcare: One health post and a private clinic.

Culture: Predominantly Oromo, known for traditional coffee ceremonies and vibrant local markets.
